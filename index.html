Research
My research interest is in computer vision through two directions: (1) how the behaviors of large nonlocal models intersect with the computational mechanisms underlying human perception, and (2) how principled human insight can steer the development of more efficient and interpretable models. I believe that answering these questions will provide fundamental insight towards developing artificial systems with human level intelligence (AGI).
